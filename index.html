Something @bout Me!

When you read my title "Interactive Media Designer" might wonder what the hell is that. IMD is a person who is comfortable with the projects either in Media or Design industry. He can work on Website Designing, Graphic Design, Photography, Photo-Editing and Videography or any media related project.So that the kind of person i am. I wouldn't say i am expert in it but i enjoy working in this field so my final work wouldn't disappoint you at all. So, what you think of working togather....... not sure.... let me tell you about my story so it might help you make your decision...

So i born in small Town of Punjab which is a northwest province of India. My town known for its holy and sacred worship places. I grew up doing Art and Craft. Not that expert but was able to draw an Apple with a green leaf with seeing a referance. As i grew older i stopped doing drawing... because my interest got build up in Craft work and more or less into painting.

Time kept on passing and my interest kept on changing from drawing to painting to craft to Digital Graphics to Animation. By the time i was in Senior Year of my High School i was already awarded with "Animator Of the Year" for continuosly 3 consecitive years. After High school i decided i will stay in this field so i took Interactive Media Design Program at ALgonquin and then here I am infront of you .... coded in HTML markups telling my lifestory which you don't even want to read but still reading in hope of finding something inspiring or funny.
